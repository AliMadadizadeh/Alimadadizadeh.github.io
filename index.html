About Us

Madadiz Inc. is a sustainability-driven engineering and consulting firm specializing in building energy performance, retrofit optimization, and decarbonization strategies for Canadian communities. Our mission is to help governments, organizations, and homeowners reduce energy costs, lower emissions, and build healthier, more resilient living environments.

With expertise in energy modeling, multi-objective optimization, and climate-responsive design, we deliver data-driven solutions that balance affordability, comfort, and sustainability. We combine scientific research, engineering insight, and practical experience to turn complex challenges into clear, actionable strategies.

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT IDEAS

At Madadiz Inc., we believe great ideas are the foundation of meaningful progress. Every innovative concept has the power to reshape buildings, communities, and the way people live. We transform ideas into practical, sustainable solutions that reduce energy use, cut emissions, and improve quality of life.

We combine technical expertise with creativity to turn challenges into opportunities. Whether it’s optimizing building performance or designing low-carbon solutions, we turn forward-thinking concepts into real-world impact

We believe that great ideas drive sustainable change. By merging innovative thinking with advanced analytics, we help create buildings that are efficient, resilient, and accessible for everyone. Our work is rooted in the belief that every idea—big or small—can contribute to a cleaner, brighter future.

Our Services

At Madadiz Inc., we provide expert engineering and sustainability consulting to help clients design, retrofit, and manage high-performance, low-carbon buildings. Our services combine technical knowledge, data-driven analysis, and practical solutions tailored to diverse Canadian climate zones.

Building Energy Modeling

We use advanced simulation tools to assess building performance, energy consumption, and retrofit potential across diverse Canadian climate zones.

Retrofit Optimization & Design

Data-driven recommendations for cost-effective, low-carbon upgrades that improve efficiency, comfort, and affordability.

Decarbonization Pathway Planning

Long-term strategies to help governments, organizations, and homeowners reduce emissions and meet climate targets.

Energy-Poverty Mitigation Solutions

Customized programs and retrofit packages designed to lower energy burdens for vulnerable and low-income households.

HVAC and Mechanical System Analysis

Performance evaluation and optimization of heating, cooling, and ventilation systems for improved comfort and efficiency.

Policy & Program Support

Technical insights and analytical support for policymakers developing sustainable building initiatives and incentive programs.

Building Performance Benchmarking

Comprehensive assessments to compare building performance with industry standards and identify improvement opportunities.

Sustainable Design & Consulting

Guidance for creating energy-efficient, climate-resilient, and environmentally responsible buildings from concept to implementation.
